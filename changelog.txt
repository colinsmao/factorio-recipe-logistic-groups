Version: 1.0.1
Date: 13. 06. 2025
  Features:
    - Support copying to and from ghost entities.
  Gui:
    - Added flying text when changing recipe logistic group ingredients, to clarify that recipe groups being unchangable is the desired behaviour.
    - Changed default alternate paste keybind to Shift + Control + Click (from Shift + Alt + Click), so it's less likely to accidentally toggle alt mode.
  Locale:
    - Fixed some locale key errors.

Version: 1.0.0
Date: 14. 11. 2024
  Major Features:
    - Initial release.
    - Create logistic groups for recipe ingredients when pasting into a requester chest. Supports quality.
    - Adds the option to add ingredients to existing requests, instead of replacing them.
    - Adds configurable multiplier options, including based on crafting time, or by product count.
    - Adds an alternate paste mode keybind, and configurable paste options for both keybinds.
